# ORBIT HOARDER
> One tap = one satellite. Ring the core, catch the debris, don't let a single piece slip through.

## Short description (CrazyGames, ~140자)
Tap to place orbiting satellites and intercept incoming debris before it reaches your core. Simple to start, brutal to master.

## Full description
ORBIT HOARDER is a one-button arcade game about building a defense grid, one tap at a time. Every tap drops a satellite into orbit around your core — but where you tap matters: the distance from center sets the orbit's radius, and the angle sets where it starts. Debris drifts in from every edge of the screen, and it's your satellites' job to intercept it before it reaches the core.

The catch is energy: launching a satellite costs energy, and the price climbs the more satellites you already have in play. Collecting debris refills your energy, so the loop feeds itself — but only if your orbits are actually covering the incoming lanes. Miss too much debris and you lose one of your three lives; lose all three and it's game over, tap to restart.

Controls are dead simple — tap anywhere to place a satellite (or restart after a game over) — but reading the debris pattern and choosing where to aim your next orbit is where the real skill lives. Screen shake, score pop, and crunchy synthesized SFX keep every catch and every miss feeling immediate.

## Controls
- Tap anywhere on screen to launch a satellite into orbit (tap position = orbit size + starting angle)
- Tap anywhere to restart after game over
